Help & Support

Welcome to the Culinary Connect Help page. Here, you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and guidance on how to navigate and make the most out of our platform.

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Enter your details including your name, email, and password. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

2. How can I explore recipes?

Once logged in, browse through our wide range of recipes by selecting a cuisine from the top menu or use the search bar to find specific dishes. You can view the recipe instructions, order the dish, or watch video tutorials to learn the cooking process.

3. Can I order food through Culinary Connect?

Yes! You can order your favorite recipes by clicking on the "Order" button next to the recipe. We partner with local restaurants to deliver dishes right to your door.

4. How can I watch recipe tutorials?

To watch a recipe tutorial, simply navigate to the recipe page and click on "Watch Tutorial." You'll get step-by-step instructions from professional chefs to guide you through the process.

5. How do I contact support?

If you need assistance, visit our Contact page to get in touch with our support team. We're here to help!

6. How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

7. Where can I find more information about Culinary Connect?

Visit our About page to learn more about our mission, vision, and services.
